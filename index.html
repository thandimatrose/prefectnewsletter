Hi everyone!

The prefects would love to introduce our newest event:
Battle of the Beat!

We challenge you to create music of any kind and any genre and put your musical abilities in the spotlight. You can use any available platform not only Garageband.

You can enter under these three categories:
✻ Original Song - This is the perfect section for our composers and singer-songwriters. All submissions in this category are original works by the artists. We’re looking forward to listening to full-fledged symphonies, people.
✻ Cover - This is for renditions and reimaginings of already released songs. We all love a classic Riptide cover. Note that remixes fall under this category as well.
✻ Open - This category is for every and anything else: jingles, beats, parody etc. This category is all about creativity and catchiness.

All submissions need to meet the criteria of being:
✻ between 20 seconds and 5 minutes long
✻ of a MP3, WAV or AIF audio format
✻ devoid of any inappropriate themes or explicit language (we’re keeping it PG, folks)

Failure to adhere to any of these criteria will result in immediate disqualification.

Submit on the following Google Form
There is also a scannable QR code on the poster.

Finally, we really want this to be an exploration of the wonders of music, the stories that can be told, the feelings that can be conveyed and just the fun that can be had in its creation.

Good luck and remember: we’re in the making of a new-age Drake :)
